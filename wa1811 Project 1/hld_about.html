About The Event

This Holiday Bashment is back for the 27th year for another year loads of fun and exciting festivities to expericence. The Bashment starts begins at 8 till Chirstmas Morning with the FrostiRun 5K and 10K races. Sign up now or the morning of the race. Remember to come early before all the spots and positions are full.

Family fun is available at the Kid's Korner with booths and free activities for the whole family, including performances from clowns, jugglers, and the wheel walkers of Dominic J.

Here to kickoff the party all the way from the Undergrounds of UK House Music Both DJK 13 & DJ EJ will be spinning on the ones & twos all night with hits of the past and the now of today and loads of remixes just waiting to be heard

The fun continues with A Table full of Holiday goodies to choose from like Honey Chicken Wings, Empanadas, Saguages, Hotdog sliders, and much more to choose and the artistically inclined should plan on attending the Winter Art Fair at Central Park

Music from Lester's Blues Band and The Jazz Express brings us into the New Year. The dancing starts at 7 p.m. and continues until midnight. From 10 to 11 p.m. we say goodbye to the old year (and ring in the new) with the famous New Year's Eve Bash fireworks spectacular.
